About Us

Welcome to pawmenow.com, a heartfelt blog dedicated to all things dogs. Founded in 2021, pawmenow is the culmination of my passion for writing and my deep love for our four-legged friends.

Pawmenow was inspired by my beloved Coco, a remarkable medium Poodle who blessed my life for an incredible 17 and a half years. Coco left a void in my heart, but he also triggered a spark within me to create a website that celebrates the bond between humans and dogs.
