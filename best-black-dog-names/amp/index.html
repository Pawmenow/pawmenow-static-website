From the classic black Labrador to the stunning great Dane, to the German Shepherd, the Rottie to the Doodle, or any adorable mixed-breed dog that acquired the dark fur genes there are lots of great names for your black dog.

When choosing the name, consider its size, appearance, and personality. From a sweet Muffin to a powerful Cosmos, there's something for every dog on this list. These names were picked from the pawmenow.com collection of dog names that contains unique black dog names for males and females, along with some badass black dog names.

Despite the stereotyping of black dogs, where some people believe that black dogs are aggressive, I think black is one of the most powerful colors for dogs. So, if you've got a new black dog that needs a name, have a look at this list of creative and powerful black dog names.

How to choose a perfect name for your black pup?

If you're having difficulties picking, remember that a beautiful black dog name can come from a variety of sources, including your favorite celebrity, food, book, movie, origin, or even canine breed history. For example, a black Labrador puppy can be named Coffee after your favorite drink, or Inky after its appearance. Another powerful dog name for your fearless pup could be Panthere or Puma.
