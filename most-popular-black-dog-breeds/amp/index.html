Large black dog breeds

1. Great Dane

This black giant is a german dog breed, also called "gentle giant" is very affectionate, devoted, and enjoys being around its family, often trying to cuddle and be a lap dog.

Did you know that besides this full black color, Great Dane comes with 9 different colors of coat recognized by AKC? The Great Dane's different coat colors are: black, black and white, white (rare color), blue, fawn, brindle, mantle, merle, harlequin.

Pure black Great Danes are not as common or as easy to find as the other colors.

The black Great Dane is usually born when two black Great Danes are mated. However, black with black can also born the blue Great Dane if one or both parents have the "blue gene". Many people wish for a pure black giant dog, but actually this color is rare and the majority black Great Danes are born with some white fur on the body, commonly on the chest and toes.

Why is pure black coat preferred on Great Dane? Because the black shines and glistens, revealing every muscle in the giant dog. The black sheen has been shown to reflect light and give the impression that the dog is extremely muscular.

Throughout history, Great Dane has been used to hunt enormous and dangerous game such as wolves, wild boars, and even bears. They are now docile family pets who have lost all traces of their hunting instinct. They also get along with other dogs and pets.

2. Cane Corso

With origin from Italy, the Cane Corso was bred to guard properties and hunt huge animals, and at first appearance, this muscular and protective dog appears threatening. Cane Corso, on the other hand, is a loyal and loving breed who is committed to his human group once you get to know him. The Cane Corso is an excellent family dog because of these mentioned qualities.

The coat of the Cane Corso is short and rigid, with a little undercoat which makes it waterproof. It comes in a variety of colors, including black, gray, red, and fawn, and may or may not have a brindle pattern.

Are black Cane Corso rare? The most common color of Cane Corsos is black. According to studies, two black parents will create a litter in which over 70% of the pups will be black. Black is also a famous color.

3. Newfoundland

The Newfoundland is a large working dog with heavy bones and a dignified temperament. The Newfie is a well-known good companion who has earned a notoriety as a gentle and vigilant "nanny dog" for children.

Beside its beautiful black coat, the sweet Newfoundland comes in some other varieties such gray, brown, and a black-and-white.

The most significant quality is its pleasant disposition. The Newf's good nature is seen in their affection towards children. Newfs are trusting and trainable, and they react well to gentle guidance. These gentle giants are among the world's largest dogs.

4. Labrador Retriever

Labrador Retrievers are among the most popular dog breeds, due to their outstanding personality and devotion for people and other animals. Aside from these positive characteristics, Labs deserve to be at the top of the list because of their intelligence.

Black Labs are known for being lively, pleasant, and devoted. They form deep bonds with their families, but they also like making new friends. A black Labrador's lifespan is 12 years, and with the appropriate food, exercise, and a fair dose of luck, your dog could live even longer.

What is the most common color of Labrador Retrievers? The most common color of Labrador is black, due to the way their color genes are inherited. Black Labradors are popular as hunting dogs, but they also make excellent pets, friends, and agility dogs.

5. Black German Shepherd

German Shepherds (GSD) are German dog breeds that, with proper training and socialization, can be extremely gentle friends and family protectors. Due to their calm nature and caring behavior, they are considered great home pups.

Being medium to large-sized breed, GSD is perfect for active families, and its intelligence and protective nature make it an excellent choice for families with youngsters (as long as the dog is properly trained).

Its colors are pretty variated (15 to be more accurate), but the common ones are:

  • black
  • black and tan
  • black and silver
  • brown
  • gray
  • blue
  • sable
  • red and black

The black German Shepherd is a pure dog breed that is distinguished by its 100% black coat. Black German Shepherds are quite rare, and as a result, their prices are substantially higher. You should anticipate to give between $700 and $2000 for a black Shepherd puppy.

Because black is a hereditary trait, both parents must have it to produce a solid black puppy. Owners of solid lack dogs are incredibly proud of their dog's color, which many refer to as "rare" because a solid black dog is not always possible to make.

Solid black puppies are born with no tan on their bodies and are entirely black. Small areas of white (on the breast or on the paws) may be present at birth, but they will fade as the puppy grows.

How rare are black German Shepherds? The black German Shepherds are extremely rare. In fact, only about 6.8% of GS puppies are born black, according to statistics.

Since German Shepherds are such smart dogs, they form the majority of police dogs around the world. They are an intelligent dog breed that can quickly understand what you want them to perform.

6. Belgian Sheepdog - Groenendael

The Belgian Sheepdog (Groenendael, in Europe) is a natural defensive and territorial breed, but when properly socialized, they can do well with kids and make ideal family pets as long as their exercise requirements are fulfilled.

Belgian Sheepdogs are watchful, loyal, and protective. They're also quite affectionate and sensitive, making them good family companions. They do, however, require a lot of activity and cerebral stimulation to remain happy.

The Belgian Shepherd dog is the only breed in the world that comes in 4 varieties:

  • the long-haired black Groenendael
  • the long-haired fawn, red or grey Tervueren
  • the short-coated red, fawn or grey Malinois
  • the rarer rough-coated reddish fawn Laekenois

The Groenendael is solid black or black with a bit of white between the pads of the feet, on the tips of the hind toes, or a small patch or strip on the forechest.

Is Groenendael only black colored? Yes, the Groenendael is solid black or black with a little patch or strip of white between the footpads, on the points of the back toes, or on the forechest.

8. Pomeranian

The small Pomeranian has been prefered as an ideal companion by both royal and common people. ¬†The Pom has become one of the most popular toy breeds in the world due to its beautiful coat, charming, foxy appearance, and energetic nature.

The Pomeranian is a small dog breed with a dominating big-dog personality and it weighs no more than 6 pounds. The voluminous double coat, with its frill reaching across the chest and shoulders, is present in nearly two dozen colors, as well as a variety of patterns and markings, but is most usually seen in orange or red.

9. Scottish Terrier

Scottish Terriers are frequently described as a large dog trapped inside a small dog's body. They do not require as much attention from their owners as other dogs. They are lovely home pets for individuals who enjoy their somewhat odd personalities and can provide gentle but firm treatment.

Scotties, like most terrier breeds, are extremely intelligent. However, they can be quite self-reliant and even disdainful when it comes to training. One of the qualities that Scottish breeders sought in a great terrier was the ability to work things out for itself.

While most people think of Scottish Terriers as black, they can also be grey, steel, brindle, or wheaten.

10. Miniature Schnauzer

The Miniature Schnauzer, the smallest and most popular of the three Schnauzer breeds, is a healthy, long-lived, and hypoallergenic companion. Mini Schnauzers are hypoallergenic, due to their lack of shedding.

With an enthusiastic disposition, friendly and charming Mini Schnauzer is ideal as a family dog.

Miniature Schnauzers are smaller versions of their larger counterparts, Standard Schnauzers. Minis has a beautiful, human-like appearance thanks to his shaggy beard and eyebrows.

Salt and pepper, black and silver, and pure black are the three color patterns present on the rough, wiry coat.
