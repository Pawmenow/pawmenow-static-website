How many treats can a French Bulldog have?

Since French Bulldogs are small in size, it's important not to overindulge them with treats. Typically, 1-3 treats a day is a safe range for a Frenchie.

Consider the treats to be like little rewards for when your dog is good, or when you just simply want to cheer it up. The amount of treats shouldn't be more than 10% of all the food your French Bulldog eats in a day. So, let's say your Frenchie needs 400 calories daily, and the treats you give them are 20 calories each. That means they can enjoy two treats each day (just an example).

Always monitor your dog's weight and adjust their treat intake accordingly. Here, your vet can guide you better.

What Snacks Are Safe for French Bulldogs?

Safe snacks for French Bulldogs include fruits like apples and berries, vegetables like carrots and green beans, and lean meats like chicken. Always avoid giving them foods that are toxic to dogs, such as grapes, onions, and chocolate.
