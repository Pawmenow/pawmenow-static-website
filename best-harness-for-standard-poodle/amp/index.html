What size harness does a Standard Poodle wear?

Given that Standard Poodles are indeed large-sized dogs, it may seem intuitive to select a large harness. However, harness sizing is not solely based on a dog's size; it involves specific measurements for a secured choice.

It's important to get the sizing right. Even though Standard Poodles come in a standard size, there can still be variations in their measurements. To find the perfect harness size, follow these steps:

  1. Measure your Poodle's girth: Using a soft measuring tape, measure around your Poodle's chest just behind the front legs. Make sure it's snug but not tight.
  2. Measure your Poodle's neck: Measure around the base of your Poodle's neck.
  3. Weight matters, too: Keep your Poodle's weight in mind, as it can impact their comfort and mobility in a harness.

Once you have these measurements, refer to the manufacturer's sizing chart for the specific harness you're considering. Sizes can vary between brands, so always consult the provided guidelines for the best fit.
