What type of bed do French Bulldogs like?

French Bulldogs really like snuggling and stretching out when they relax or get ready for a nap. So, the perfect bed for a Frenchie would be one that's shaped like an oval with comfy, supportive bolsters to make sure it gets a good night's sleep no matter how it likes to rest. And if it's orthopedic, it's even better to give your pup extra comfort.

💁🏼‍♀️ A bit of context for the orthopedic beds for French Bulldogs

Orthopedic beds are the best choice to purchase for any Frenchie, but especially for seniors who suffer from arthritis and sore joints.

The major component of such a dog bed should be memory foam because it conforms to the dog's body. In other words, a Frenchie will feel like he is lying on the coziest and softest bed on the planet.

What size bed does a French Bulldog need?

The perfect size bed a French Bulldog needs is one that ensures your dog can comfortably fit in any position, whether it's curled up like a ball or stretched out like a starfish across the entire bed. 😅

To determine the correct size bed for your Frenchie, measure it while it is sleeping. This is essential in establishing the proper bed size.

Usually, a French Bulldog is about 11 to 12 inches tall. Males weigh between 20 and 28 pounds, while females weigh between 16 and 24 pounds.
