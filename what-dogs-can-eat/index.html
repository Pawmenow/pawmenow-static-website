There are numerous viewpoints on this nutrition topic. That is why, if you want to know whether or not a particular food can be consumed by your dog, you need to do research on more than one source.

But for sure, you've been in a position where your dog was staring at you eagerly as you were eating, badly seeking a bite of your meal.

That’s why, this app did the research for you, to sum up in a few words what foods are good or bad for your dog to eat.

！Please keep in mind that this app and all written content are not medical guidance, tho'. Many factors are dependent on your dog's health, so contact your vet first.

Even if food is permitted for dogs, it should be given in moderation and as an occasional treat rather than as a regular part of their diet. Never replace traditional dog food with human food.

Because every dog is different, start with a small amount of the "allowed food" to ensure your dog can digest it. Before adding a new food into your dog's diet, always discuss with your vet.
