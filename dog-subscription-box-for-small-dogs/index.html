5 Best Dog Subscription Box for Small Dogs [2024 Top Picks]

Pawmenow

09 Jan 2024 7 min

Dog parents all over the world like pampering their furry friends. Even though nothing compares to giving our dogs much love, cuddles, and kisses, certain dogs enjoy treats and toys so much that they could convince us by some blinks of puppy eyes to spoil them with the best toys and treats. But, what can we do? We could not blame them for that.

A new trend in the pet market has just begun to gain popularity, namely box subscriptions for dogs of all sizes that make our pups happy.

This article will treat the small dog breed subscription boxes subject, including what they are, and why they're worthwhile to pay for and also we'll expose the best on the market.

üéÅ What are dog subscription boxes?

Well, these boxes can be shortly described as an entertaining monthly "treat" for your dog.

Inside this box, the subscription can be toys and treats for your dog, that can be personalized depending on what your dog prefers or depending on its personality.

For example, if your dog is a heavy chewer this box could contain chewy toys along with some good and healthy treats alike.

Why do people prefer monthly subscription boxes

During the COVID-19 era, this service became so well-liked that despite regulations, boxes were safely delivered to customers' homes without raising a finger.

Pet owners immediately fell in love with this idea and applied for monthly subscriptions because it was so well-designed and well-liked by dogs.

Are dog subscription boxes worth it?

The answer could be yes or no depending on you and your dog.

The concept is okay, and it's simple to make the ideal present for a dog, according to the PROs. Additionally, for the people with an affording budget, why not invest in something without considering how much it would cost at the end of the year?

On the other hand, we could not ignore the potential repercussions. If your dog isn't all that impressed, what then? Or would you consider it a wise investment? But these should be taken into account as being subjective. It is essential to conduct thorough research before selecting a subscription box. Additionally, if you are aware that your dog prefers treats to toys, a toy box may not be the best option.

Ok, now that you already have a somehow opinion, let's see what are the best dog boxes for our little tiny dogs.
