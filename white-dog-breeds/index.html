9 Best Pure White Dog Breeds: Exclusively White by Nature

Looking for a dog with a strikingly beautiful and exclusively white coat? Check out these best pure white dog breeds! In this article, we'll introduce you to these breeds and give you some insight into their personalities. Keep reading to find your perfect white companion. ü§ç

There are hundreds of white dog breeds globally, but only a few breeds that are exclusively white by nature. Many breeds can have white coats, but the same breeds can have many other color combinations.

Most people think about white dog breeds as small fluffy dogs, ¬†such as Bicon Frise or Maltese. Actually, white dog breeds come in all shapes and sizes. From small white dog breeds to big white dog breeds and from white short-haired dog breeds to fluffy white dog breeds.

I've selected for you the most popular white dog breeds that are absolutely irresistible.

White dog breeds exclusively white by nature

Here are all the pure white dog breeds that you will surely love - whether they are fluffy, small, or large! ü§ç

1. Samoyed: The Fluffiest White Dog Breed

This beautiful white cloud deserves its place at the top of the list.

The charming Samoyed is a large, white fluffy breed. Looking angelic and like walking clouds, Samoyeds were actually working dogs in the Arctic with sled-pulling and hunting duties. This means that this dog breed is not a couch potato, but a highly active dog with lots of human interaction needed. Also, his long and fluffy coat is for keeping him warm in freezing conditions, not only for a charming look.

This fluffy white coat needs some special treatment, though. Being so damn beautiful takes hard work!

Another thing you must know about Samoyeds is that they make wonderful family pets. They are also among the most expensive dog breeds.

Samoyed is the most expensive white dog, reaching in 2021 the cost of $14.000.

2. Great Pyrenees: The Largest White Dog Breed

Great Pyrenees is the largest white dog breed and among the most patient dogs. This gentle giant can weigh 100 pounds or even more and he can grow to 32 inches tall.

Great Pyrenees is also known as the "mountain dog" from the mountain range in southwestern Europe. This dog breed has been used as a guardian of the flocks. Since then he acquired his name after being brought to the Pyrenees mountains.

This doggo is one of the most affectionate dog breeds. He is strong-willed and fearless, a confident and docile dog for families.

If you want to have this giant as a lifetime friend, be prepared for so much fur around along with shedding and brushing sessions.

3. ¬†Dogo Argentino: The White Short-Haired Dog Breed

Among large white short-haired dog breeds, stands Dogo Argentino, a massive dog with a smooth white coat. Though, he has a single dark patch over an ear or eye.

He is a powerful muscular dog breed, created to fight big predators such as mountain lions and wild boars.

Along with his strength is a loyal and affectionate family dog, especially kind and gentle with kids.

Dogo Argentino's white coat doesn't shed a lot. He is one of the white dog breeds easy to groom. But, you have to beware of sunlight exposure because they are prone to sunburn due to their short white coat.

4. Maltese: The Smallest White Dog Breed

Maltese is the smallest white dog breed. This cute fluff stays under 10 inches tall.

He has a long, smooth white coat that makes him an irresistible dog and famous among small companion dogs. The grooming part can take some time investment to keep him styled. ¬†

The cute Maltese is perfect for those looking for a hypoallergenic small white dog. Why? Because his long and smooth coat rarely sheds.

As most small dog breeds they are fearless and prone to bark uncontrollably. They are very curious and dominant, but also very loving with people and kids.

5. Bichon Frise: The Small Curly White Dog Breed

A walking bubble bath? A barking fluffy cloud? or maybe a cute four legs "whipped cream" ? NOO, it is just a Bichon Frise. With its fluffy, pure white coat and round black eyes like bottoms, this dog breed looks more like a stuffed animal than a real dog. ¬†

Bichon Frise is among the most popular dog breeds loved by families and kids. It is was also a toy dog for French aristocrats. So, this pup is a fancy small curly dog breed that everybody loves.

Always cheerful and highly active it is believed to be descended from the poodle dog breed.

Bichon Frises are also prone to bad behavior like destructive habits and excessive barks if they are not properly trained.

His beautiful coat requires lots of maintenance with regular brush and haircuts.

Other pure white large dog breeds:

White Golden Retrievers. Actually, this breed comes in many color varieties of golden, but Goldens can be also born with a beautiful pure white coat.

White Siberian Husky. This beautiful sled dog breed comes also in a pure white coat color.

Standard Poodle. This curly-haired dog breed comes in 4 size varieties and many colors: cream, brown, apricot, grey, black, cafe ou lait. White is the most popular color for a poodle.

Solid White Doberman. It is uncommon to see a cream or ivory-colored Doberman, and neither the AKC nor the UKC approves them. These creamy white canines, sometimes known as partial albinos, have a disorder called leucism that results in a partial lack of pigmentation.

Are pure white dogs rare?

It depends on the breed, but pure white dogs are not rare. While white may be an uncommon color variation, some breeds are more likely than others to have white coats.
