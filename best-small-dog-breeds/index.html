Small dog breeds aren't always calm dogs. The smallest dog breeds are often big dogs in small bodies, fearless and vocal. Those cute small dog breeds have a reputation for making their voice known by their barking.

But who could resist their charm? Especially when they are so small you could take them everywhere you want. They can be the perfect couch potato with lots of snuggles to give.

Chihuahuas are also called pocket dogs. That means that they are so small you could take them anywhere you want.
