Curly dog breeds are among the most popular dog breeds due to their stylish and appealing appearance.

Dog lovers prefer a curly-haired dog breed not only because they look pretty, but because their coats are revealing some advantages:

  • Most curly dog breeds are hypoallergenic, which means they don't shed or shed less than a usual breed.
  • Water-repellant double-coat makes the dog ideal for swimming in cold water.

Color plays an important role in choosing a dog. Every pet lover will choose a color that fits his personality. The most popular colors are solid ones. Here's the ranking after some research:

  1. Black dogs
  2. White dogs
  3. Brown dogs

As we maybe didn't expect, black dog breeds are at the top of the list because they're the most common. Then comes the white dog breeds, which we probably thought to be in the first place. Particularly white curly dog breeds, which include the most recognizable breed: the Poodle, have uniqueness. Therefore, third place goes to brown curly dog breeds, which are nevertheless gorgeous.

In this article, you'll find the most popular brown curly-haired dogs, along with their characteristics. So, be sure you stick with me until the end. You'll be in love with them!

How do you take care of a curly dog coat?

If you have a dog with a curly coat, you must be aware that your pet needs a regular hair care routine in order to keep his coat or hair curly.

You can add the following tips to your dog's normal grooming routine to help keep his curls in good condition:

1. Use a slicker brush in sections

2. Lookout for tangling

3. Brush the entire body

4. Trim as needed

5. Regularly fluff, clip, & bathe
