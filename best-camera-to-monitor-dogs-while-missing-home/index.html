Pawmenow

24 Jan 2023 8 min

We are all aware that the most difficult aspect of owning a dog is remaining apart from it, particularly while leaving him alone at home.

Most dogs are prone to separation anxiety, which is why it is essential to train your pup from a young age to cope with being alone at home for a few hours.

A fantastic approach to eliminate a lot of worries is to use a camera to monitor your puppy while you are away from home.

On the market, there are numerous cameras to select from, each with a unique set of modern features such as voice control, 360-degree tracking, real-time motion, and much more.

Do pet cameras help with separation anxiety?

Yes, but not entirely. A monitoring camera will be more helpful for you to observe how your dog deals with separation anxiety. Some professional cameras with features like interactions with food and voice may ease your pooch's anxiety.

What you should really take into consideration is to train your dog to well behave and feel safe when remaining home alone. Another condition is to prepare him in the best conditions to feel relaxed.

Usually, all dogs are prone to separation anxiety if they're not properly trained. Some breeds are more exposed to it than others. For example, poodles - the one-person dogs. You can read more about this anxiety issue in Poodles available in all dogs).

How can I watch my dog when I'm not at home?

When you're not at home, there are a number of ways to keep an eye on your dog, including employing pet-specific camera systems, smartphone apps, smart gadgets, hiring a dog walker or sitter, or using a pet-sitting service.

These choices can provide you comfort and let you to check on your dog from a distance, but it's crucial to remember that they cannot take the place of human attention and care. If you're going to be gone for a while, it's ideal to have someone physically there to watch over your dog.

Is there a security camera that works without WiFi?

Yes, actually it is. After a short research, I found Arlo Go. When traveling or in locations with little or no WiFi access, the Arlo Go Mobile Security Camera is the appropriate security monitoring solution. Arlo Go may be used everywhere because it supports 3G/4G LTE wireless connectivity.

🗣 Should I talk to my dog through the camera?

Speaking with your dog through the camera while you're away should normally help to alleviate the anxiety situations.

If your dog always responds to your voice, a pet monitoring camera is an excellent solution for separation anxiety. You could check your dog during the day and tell it you'll be back home soon.

❕
Observation: In some situations, using a voice controller camera can make a dog more anxious. If you notice that your dog becomes more agitated when he hears you, avoid speaking to him over the camera's speakers.

💰 How much does a pet cam cost?

A monitoring camera for pets can start at lower prices under $20, but a good one can start from $50 up to $300-$400. More professional cams can easily exceed $500.
