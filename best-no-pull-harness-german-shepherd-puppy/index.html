Are no pull harnesses good for puppies?

Yes, for puppies, no-pull collars can be an excellent solution, especially if they have a tendency to tug on their leash while being walked. By teaching your puppy to walk calmly on a leash and preventing dragging, using a no-pull harness can make walks more pleasurable for both you and your puppy.

So it's crucial to pick a harness that's made especially for puppies and that fits them appropriately. Puppies grow quickly, so it's important to check that the harness fits properly and to make any necessary adjustments. Choose a harness that won't irritate your puppy's sensitive skin and is composed of soft, lightweight materials as well.

As with any training tool, it's crucial to be patient with your puppy as it learns to walk on a leash and to apply positive reinforcement tactics. A no-pull harness can be a useful tool for teaching your puppy excellent leash manners and ensuring its safety on walks if it is used in combination with proper training and equipment.
