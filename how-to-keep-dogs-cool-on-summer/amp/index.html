Beat the Heat: How to Keep Dogs Cool in the Summer?

Pawmenow - 17 Apr 2023

During the summer, when temperatures start to increase, it is crucial to keep your dog cool and hydrated. It is especially important to take the required precautions to keep dogs safe because they are prone to heatstroke and other heat-related disorders. Because some breeds are more prone to overheating than others, it is best to monitor your dog's behavior and take the appropriate precautions to keep them cool.

How to keep dogs cool in the summer?

1. Give your dog a lot of water and shade
A shady space should be created for your dog to relax in, and you should make sure it always have access to clean, fresh water. Be careful not to leave your dog outside for too long without access to water or shade.

2. Freeze treats

Freeze some of your dog's favorite treats, such as peanut butter or yogurt, in ice cube trays or molds.This will not only give it a nice treat, but it will also assist in cooling it off.

3. Use cooling mats or beds

Cooling mats or beds are made to keep your dog cool and also comfortable, even on the hottest days.These mats employ technology with gel infusion to absorb and dissipate heat, giving your dog a cool surface to lay on.

4. Avoid exercising while it is hot outside
Exercise during the hottest part of the day can be dangerous for your dog. Opt for early morning or evening walks when temperatures are cooler.

5. Keep your dog groomed
Keeping your dog well-groomed might help it stay cool during the heat. Your dog's body temperature can be regulated and kept from rising too high with the aid of a well-groomed coat.

6. Cool your dog in a kiddie pool
Your dog can splash around in a little kiddie pool that has been filled with chilly water. This is a simple and entertaining technique to keep your dog amused and cool.

Dogs love splashing the water in the pool! Make your dog happy by getting him a pool in the garden or going to a special pool for dogs.

7. Use a cooling vest
Cooling vests are designed to help regulate your dog's body temperature and keep them cool while out on walks or during other outdoor activities. These vests can be soaked in water and then worn by your dog to help lower their body temperature.

How can I keep my dog cool without AC?

There are various ways to keep your dog cool even without access to air conditioning. Fans, a lot of water, shade, and cooling mats or mattresses are all options. You can also use cooling clothing and freeze dog treats.

What temperature is too hot for a dog outside?

Dogs are prone to overheating, and they can become dangerously ill at temperatures of more than 90 degrees Fahrenheit. Avoid exercising your dog during the hottest part of the day and always keep it cool.

What is the fastest way to cool down a dog?

Wetting your dog with cool (not cold) water and setting them in front of a fan are the quickest ways to help them cool off if they are overheating. Additionally, you can wipe their feet and belly with cool towels.

How long can a dog stay outside in 90-degree weather?

Dogs should not spend a lot of time outside when it is hotter than 90 degrees Fahrenheit. Make sure they have access to shade and lots of drink if they must be outside.

Conclusion  ☀️

As responsible dog owners, it is our responsibility to make sure that during the hot summer months, our four-legged pets remain cool and comfortable. You can keep your dog safe and guard against heat-related illnesses like heatstroke by using the advice in this article.

Keep in mind to continually monitor your dog's behavior and take the appropriate precautions to keep it hydrated and cool. Keep cool and have fun this summer with your dog!
