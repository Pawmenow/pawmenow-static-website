Curly white dogs are the sweetest dogs that steal everyone's eyes! Besides their good-looking appearance, some of them are hypoallergenic. What could possibly be better than this?

According to the American Kennel Club, several curly-haired breeds were evolved as water dogs, assisting fishermen or helping in water rescue, or as herders in wet and cold environments. At that time, there was no emphasis on grooming and caring for fur, but nowadays most curly-haired dog breeds require a significant amount of maintenance to keep their coats clean and healthy.

Many curly-haired breeds are either non-shedders, hypoallergenic, or both for allergy-prone families.

This article will present you some of the most gorgeous white curly-haired dog breeds, from the smallest to the largest, that will undoubtedly melt your heart.

1. Toy Poodle - Intelligent small dog breed perfect for families

Poodles are among the smartest dog breeds, that are known for being one person's devoted dog and best buddy for life. They are also known as white teacup poodles.

Toy Poodles have a high level of energy and are easy to train, making them the ideal companion for active people. They're also perfect for apartment life due to their small size.

2. Bichon Frise - Loving small dog breed

Another white dog breed with curls that is famous among pet lovers is Bichon Frise.

These cute tiny dogs are fantastic companions that are both energetic and sensitive, known to get along nicely with other dogs and cats. They are also, an excellent friend to children.

Always cheerful, this small dog breed enjoys adventure and has a lot of affection to share. You may say that it looks like a child's toy because of its black eyes and fluffy white coat - a strong reason to be among favorites when it's about picking a color.

Bichons have a powder-puff coat, pretty much similar to Poodles, with silky but curly outer hair and a soft undercoat. Their color variations are white, white and buff, white and cream, white and apricot.

3. Maltipoo - Therapy dog due to its loving personality

Maltipoos are a famous mix of Maltese and a Poodle (Toy or Miniature). Like their parents, they are very loving and friendly.

Grooming overview

‚úÇÔ∏è Coat care: Medium-maintenance coat
To maintain their coat clean and clear of mats, daily brushing is a must. A Maltipoo's tail should be clipped once or twice a year, while his head should be trimmed monthly.
Maltipoos should be groomed every three weeks at most. Because their hair grows faster, Maltipoos require more attention.

The Maltipoo has a low-shedding, low-dander coat that is fluffy, velvety, and wool-like. It's medium to long in length and wavy to curly in texture. Its coat colors are white, cream, and silver.

5. Lagotto Romagnolo - The truffle dog

Lagotto Romagnolo, also known as truffle dog, is an Italian dog breed with a plush-toy look due to its curly and stylish appearance.

This dog breed is known for wooly curls that cover the body from head to tail, and like Poodles, they also have hair, not fur.

The white version of Lagotto is stunning, but did you know that this breed is among the most popular curly brown dog breeds? Actually, the brown Lagotto has become the favorite for its breed.

Grooming overview

The rough and waterproof coat of the Lagotto creates thick and tight curls that cover its full body excluding the head. With a double coat of hair instead of fur, they shed far too little, but sometimes they may leave tufts of hair.
Trimming the coat on a regular basis is required. Some of them have coats that mat more than others, so it is necessary to groom on a periodically to avoid matting. Also, be sure to check their ears frequently for wax or debris accumulation or infection.

Lagotto Romagnolo dogs are active, dedicated, and intelligent. They enjoy learning and pleasing their owners, and they thrive in dog sports like obedience and agility, as well as recreational activities such as dock diving, because they are water lovers by nature.

6. Mini Golden Doodle - Affectionate & intelligent " teddy bear"

Mini Goldendoodle or Miniature Goldendoodle is the crossbreed of a Miniature or Toy Poodle with a Golden Retriever.

The most prevalent style of Goldendoodle coat is wavy or shaggy, and they are also known as fleece coats. A fleece-coated Labradoodle is created by mixing a curly wool coat gene (Poodle) with a straight coat gene (Goldendoodle).

Grooming overview

While some Goldendoodle's coats are more like Poodles - with a beautiful, high-maintenance coat that requires frequent trimming, they must be groomed and clipped on a regular basis.
If the coat is maintained short, it should be cut and brushed every 6 to 8 weeks. If the coat is allowed to grow naturally, it should be brushed once every week or two.

Goldendoodles are friendly to everyone including people and pets. They are calm and gentle with young kids, being an excellent choice for households.

Conclusion

Dogs with curly coats generally shed less. If you are allergic and you don't want dog hair all around your home, a curly-coated dog is the best choice for you. Low-shed, curly-haired dogs, are not far from shedding at all - a little amount & loose hair gets trapped in their curly coats. This trapped hair causes knots and later will mat their curly coat if it's not routinely combed.

Be sure you understand the responsibilities of having a white curly-haired dog before getting one. As their color is light and curly, a grooming routine is a must.

In rest, you'll be the proudest dog parent to have such a beautiful fluffy dog!

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of dog has white curly hair?

In general, curly breeds are water dogs due to their Poodle, Lagotto Romagnolo, Barbet, Portuguese Water Dog, and Spanish Water Dog. Those mentioned happen to come in white variations as well.

But, besides those water dogs mentioned, Bichon Frise is a pure curly white hair dog breed.

What are the little curly white dogs called?

Usually, the first thought of a small white curly dog breed is the Bichon (Frise).

What breed of dog is white and fluffy?

There are many white fluffy dog breeds, such as: Bichon (Frise, Maltese, Havanese), Poodles, and Samoyeds. 

Do white Poodles change colors?

Yes. They might change colors as they age, or they can change colors according to certain foods they consume.

Are white dogs more prone to skin allergies?

Yes. Lighter fur dogs have less skin pigmentation, which results in lighter fur color.

Allergies are more common in white dogs. Dogs with white coats have more sun damage to their skin, putting them at a higher risk of getting skin malignancies caused by sun exposure.
